THE ADVENTURE

20+ years of advancing ecological stewardship and creating unforgettable experiences.

Travel & Tourism

At MCTT, we are proud to present the Mehloding Adventure Trail, an exciting hiking trail that winds through the foothills of the Drakensberg Mountains. Our trail offers an immersive experience in nature, where hikers can explore untouched landscapes and learn about the region's rich cultural heritage.

Mission & Values

Our vision is to promote prosperity through the development of ecotourism while preserving the natural and cultural heritage of Matatiele.

Sustainable Travel

It has been our pleasure to preserve and maintain the two existing ecotourism products, namely Masakala Guest House and Mehloding Adventure Trail, develop an efficient and functional sustainable business, identify other ecotourism products for development, provide sustainable income opportunities, improve the livelihood of rural people through sustainable employment, securing financing, managing and managing income, environmental protection and capacity building of any project.

ABOUT MEHLODING

Join our Hiking Trail, and leave a positive footprint both in nature and in communities.

Breathtaking Experience.

Through the Mehloding Adventure Trail, hikers will discover breathtaking views and peace of mind, nature waterfalls and ancient rock art sites, all contributing to our community's conservation efforts. Every step we take on our path supports sustainability initiatives, including invasive plants, trail maintenance and community empowerment.

Decades of Experience.

Established in 2002, the Mehloding Community Tourism Trust (MCTT) is a beacon of sustainability in the Eastern Cape region. Our trust was established with the aim of harnessing the potential of ecotourism for poverty alleviation while protecting the natural and cultural heritage of our region.
