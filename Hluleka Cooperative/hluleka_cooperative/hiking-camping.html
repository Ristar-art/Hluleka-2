Matatiele Heritage Event

Established in 2010 through the inspiration of the Mehloding Trust Coordinator, Tsepo Lesholu, as a partnership between Freedom Challenge and Mehloding Trails.

The event covers mountain biking, traditional music and entertainment, horse racing (trotting & fast runner), best-dressed jockey and horse competition, traditional food, fashion, and traditional attire from women of all different cultures, etc.

Main objectives of the event are to showcase our rich diversified culture in Matatiele (attire, music, food, dances), re-introduce the indigenous horse racing, encourage youth to participate in mountain biking and the importance of rock art for the coming generation. Local crafters are also given the opportunity to display their work to the many tourists who will be visiting our area.

Best Season: 21 September

Popular Location: Ditichereng Sports Ground (Moiketsi Reserve)
